Protein and banana!


Description

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana, medium
  • Milk, not the 2% nonsense
  • Oatmeal flour
  • Isolate protein powder, chocolate

Steps

  1. Peel the banana
  2. Place all the ingredients in a blender
  3. Turn on the blender!
  4. Pour into a decently-sized shaker or mason jar
  5. Enjoy
