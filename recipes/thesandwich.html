The monster sandwich


Description

Ingredients

  • Two slices of Dave's Killer Bread
  • Salami, sliced 20 gr.
  • Turkey breast, sliced 100 gr.
  • Serrano ham, 100 gr.
  • Gouda cheese
  • Manchego cheese
  • BBQ sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Add some butter to your DKB slices
  2. Put the slices on a frying pan, melt the butter
  3. Make a dressing with two parts mayo, one part BBQ sauce, and one part mustard
  4. Spread your dressing on top of the buttered DKB slice
  5. Add gouda cheese to one slice, manchego cheese to the other
  6. Add turkey breast, serrano, and salami
  7. Close your sandwich and place it on a dish
  8. Enjoy
